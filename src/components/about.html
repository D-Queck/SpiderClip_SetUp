What is SpiderClip?

SpiderClip was co-developed by the Serious Games Engineering research group at University of Kaiserslautern-Landau. This open-source framework for VR wearables has underpinned a variety of HCI and exergame studies—from real-time sensor integration to extensive usability evaluations in virtual reality fitness environments.

Its modular architecture enables researchers and developers to rapidly prototype new devices, capture raw data in real time, and seamlessly integrate with leading VR tracking systems.

Enjoy exploring SpiderClip—and feel free to get in touch with feedback or collaboration ideas!
